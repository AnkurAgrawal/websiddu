Process

Competitive analysis

To begin with I did some research on the similar products. There are many products that are similar to Google Voice and serves the same purpose. I installed most of the mobile application on my phone and tried them out to get some inspiration. There are few common things that all these application do, for example give a new number, Voice mail list, Transcripts etc...
