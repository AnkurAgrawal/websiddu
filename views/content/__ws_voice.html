Summary

This is my personal side project. I didn't build this for Google.

Process
Research

I reached out to some of my friends who uses Google voice and interviewed 4 users to understand how they use Google Voice. There are some interesting things that I found.

  • Everyone have their own way of using Google voice
  • Most of the people use Google voice because of the free calls
  • 3 out of 4 participants told that they want to get a new number as they don't want to give away their identity.
  • 1 participant told that he never used Google Voice mobile app on this phone.
  • All the participants used the Google Hangouts dialer.
  • 2 participants said that they use Google voice to read their and voice mails transcripts.
  • 1 participant said that he gets frustrated sometimes to just listen to blank voice mail.
  • All of them said getting a new number from Google voice is painful.
Design Requirements
  • Make on-boarding smooth and easy.
  • Effective way of eliminating blank voice mails.
  • Automated custom voice mails.
  • Easy way to read transcripts and reply with text.
  • Make the re-charge flow easy and less painful.
Competitive analysis

To begin with I did some research on the similar products. There are many products that are similar to Google Voice and serves the same purpose. I installed most of the mobile application on my phone and tried them out to get some inspiration. There are few common things that all these application do, for example give a new number, Voice mail list, Transcripts etc...
